RPANBot Privacy Policy

When does the bot store info?

RPANBot only stores information when it is needed to provide one of its features.

When is data removed or how can I remove it?

You can delete your stream notifications settings using a command and set the custom prefix back to the bot's default prefix.

When the bot leaves or is removed from a guild it will attempt to delete any settings it has stored for that guild.

Alternatively, you can contact the developers using the bot's feedback command, or sending a modmail message to r/RPANBot through Reddit, requesting that any settings for your guild be deleted.

What information does RPANBot store?
  • Broadcast Notification Settings
  • Custom Guild Prefixes
Is this policy prone to change?

This policy may need to change with the integration of new features into RPANBot.
